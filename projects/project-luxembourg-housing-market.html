Monthly Housing Analysis

This research presents an informed analysis of Luxembourg's housing market, derived from a methodical review of nearly 350 real estate agencies across the country. Significant time is invested in monitoring each agency and delving into the current trends of the housing market. On a regular basis, new listings are personally scrutinized, with a particular emphasis on three key aspects: price, size, and location. This deliberate approach offers a comprehensive overview of the data, aiding in the understanding of market tendencies. The project's individualized method of tracking each advertisement contributes to a detailed and nuanced view of Luxembourg's housing landscape.
